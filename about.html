About MaffiesHousing

Our Vision

Our vision is to ensure that every student that travelled miles to reach Mahikeng is placed in a secure, comfortable and affordable student housing.

We believe that great student housing inspires confidence to do great in obtaining academic success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to work hand in hand with property managers to ensure that the quality of student houses that are showcased on our website are of the perfect quality and standard.

Further ensuring that students are safe and comfortable without any hassles while chasing their dreams.
